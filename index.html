Research

I work on the topic of integrating surrounding and environmental data in energy-efficient building renovation under supervision of Prof. Dr. Timo Hartmann. With a background in Geo-informatics, I investigate the effect of surrounding and environmental data in the energy-efficient building renovation process and the multidisciplinary nature of construction projects within the context of the environment, technology, and people. With my research, I aim at taking meaningful steps toward sustainable cities by developing ideas for buildings as sustainable energy systems and increasing the resilience of buildings, and ultimately cities by pushing urban digitalisation.
